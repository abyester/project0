Improv Games 1

Wrong door - you go confident into a room only to realise it's the wrong room. Sudden drop in status as you seek to extricate yourself.
Shop - audience put items in virtual shop. Actors then act using those items.
Die! - actors need to talk seamlessly and continuously as they are pointed towards by lead. Anyone who hesitates or deviates is told to Die!
"Boris!" - one actor is policeman and the other is guilty criminal. Actor asked outlandish questions about crime (why was there a turkey baster at the scene?) and calls in imaginary policeman Boris when actor can't answer.
Typewriter - one actor is at the typewriter directing the others through different scenes.
Emotions party - audience gives each actor and emotion. As each enters party everyone picks up that emotion. As people leave last first, emotion reverts to that of the person still there who entered last.
Sexy/Smelly/Funny - each actor decides privately which of the winning other actors is Sexy, smelly, or funny. They each enter party with gifts chosen by the audience and hilarity ensues.
3 headed expert - 3 actors are one expert (expertise chosen by the audience), interviewed by the remaining actor. They need to answer questions in unison and reflect each others body language.
Change! - 2 actors act scene while other calls on them to "change" at strategic points. Fast firing the changes can cause actors to come up with things that are all the more amusing.
Endline - audience choses an endline and an occupation. Two actors have to get to that line.
Continuation - two actors start a scene (audience choses who they are) and at key point two other actors have to continue where they left off.
Little voice - one actor is on stage (location chosen by audience) when they hear voice of other actor off stage. They have to discover who voice is.
